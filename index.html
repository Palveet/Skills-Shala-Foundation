About Us


Millions of individuals are part of this education system that is very much content-driven and focuses primarily on acquiring subject matter expertise. This amplified focus on content gives Indian professionals an edge for proficiency. Even with this advantage, their development, success, and sense of well being stunted because they are unable to express and articulate their thoughts and ideas effectively.
Skills Shala Foundation was born in 2019 as a platform to give children the tools and techniques to identify their talents and gifts and develop essential capabilities and life skills.



Our Programs


Masterclass


Full-day workshop facilitated by an expert/influencer to enable the enhancement of various skills. We are currently partnering with organisations to deliver these masterclasses under a co-branded label.

Short Term Courses


We design and customize these programs according to the unique needs of a particular school or group, and work with students to develop their socio-emotional, linguistic, and overall communication skills to improve their access to higher education and employment opportunities.

Train the Trainer


Customised curriculum and content to enhance the English language teaching methodology of volunteers. After undergoing this training for three months, these volunteers, in turn, deliver a 120-hour course to learners. We are currently developing and deploying a customized ‘English for Employability’ course with curriculum, content, and learning materials for students.

Period Pratha


"The Period Pratha" is an initiative for propagating awareness on Menstruation. Our module focus on covering all issues on menstrual hygiene, a life beyond the clutches of myths and taboos.

Gyan Daan


A unique program where we urge volunteers to donate time school students and help them in learning. The program helps children to learn new things, techniques that eventually accelerates their learning.
